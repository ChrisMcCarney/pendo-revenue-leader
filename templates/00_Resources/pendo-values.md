# Pendo Values (Pank Pillars)

Pendo's four company values, used internally for recognition (Slack hashtags) and for framing internal comms.

---

## The four

1. **Maniacal Focus on the Customer** (`#ManiacalFocusOnTheCustomer`), Customer outcomes drive every decision. We obsess over the problem, not just the product.
2. **Hone Your Craft** (`#HoneYourCraft`), Continuous improvement of skills, knowledge, and execution. Mastery as a habit.
3. **Bias to Act** (`#BiasToAct`), Move with urgency. Make the call, ship, learn, iterate.
4. **The Team is Pendo** (`#TheTeamIsPendo`), We win together. Trust, transparency, lifting others.

---

## How to use them

- **Recognition:** When acknowledging a teammate on Slack, tag the relevant value. Don't force-fit; pick the one that genuinely applies.
- **Framing:** When writing internal comms, leadership updates, or recognition messages, anchor the narrative in one of the four. Pendo readers will pick up the reference.
- **Self-check:** When making a tough call, ask which value the decision lives under. If it doesn't fit any, reconsider.

---

## Common usage examples

- "Massive `#ManiacalFocusOnTheCustomer` energy from the team this week, {teammate} ran the {customer} requirements list back to them in 24 hours."
- "Big `#HoneYourCraft` moment: {teammate} shipped their first reverse-demo build."
- "`#BiasToAct`, {teammate} just moved a demo from next week to tomorrow because the CEO is in town."
- "`#TheTeamIsPendo`, when one of us wins, all of us win."

---

## Tone

The values are sincere, not performative. Don't over-use them. Don't tag yourself unless you're being self-deprecating. Don't pile multiple tags on one message.
