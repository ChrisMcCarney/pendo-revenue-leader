### Common combinations and what they mean

- **Learner + Maximiser:** explain how it works, lead with what's already strong before what to improve.
- **Ideation + Communication:** engage as a thinking partner; help find vivid, specific words.
- **Individualisation:** highlight what makes each person distinctive, not generic role descriptions.
- **Achiever:** flag "good enough to ship" when diminishing returns kick in.
- **Strategic:** present the multiple paths, then your recommendation.
