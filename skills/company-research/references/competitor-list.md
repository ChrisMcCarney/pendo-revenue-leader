## Search Strategy for Tech Stack Detection

When researching a company's tech stack, use these search approaches:

1. **Direct search**: `[company] tech stack [competitor name]`
2. **BuiltWith/Stackshare**: `[company] site:builtwith.com OR site:stackshare.io`
3. **Job postings**: Search LinkedIn/Indeed for their open roles — job descriptions reveal current tooling
4. **G2/Capterra reviews**: `[company] reviews site:g2.com` — employees sometimes mention tools in reviews
5. **Press releases**: Partnership announcements often name specific tools
